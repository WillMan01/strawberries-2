Grow Strawberries

Why Grow Strawberries

Strawberries are easy to grow even for beginners, they are nutritious and delicous, especially when you can harvest your own at home. Strawberries can grow in most climates so long as you plant them where they can take advantage of full sunlight.

Healthy Benefits

Strawberries are fat free and vitiman rich. Strawberries contain Antioxidants in the form of Ellagic acid and vitiman c which help to prevent cardiovascular problems and other inflammatory conditions. Strawberries are good for bone health and also help prevent and fight infection.
